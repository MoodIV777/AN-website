---
title: Voice AI Architecture for Call Centers
date: 2026-02-01
tags: Voice AI, ASR, TTS, RAG, LLM
---
An exploration of how voice large models integrate into customer service systems — comparing cascade (ASR→NLU→DM→TTS) vs. end-to-end voice-to-voice architectures. End-to-end models retain 75% emotional context (vs. 5% in cascade), but hybrid approaches currently offer the best trade-off between controllability and naturalness. Covers RAG-powered context retrieval, speculative decoding, and sub-200ms latency engineering.
