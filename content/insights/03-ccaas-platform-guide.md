A comparative analysis of CCaaS platforms (Genesys, NICE, Five9, Talkdesk, Amazon Connect, etc.) across deployment models, AI capabilities, and pricing. The global CCaaS market is projected to reach $27B by 2033 at 15.5% CAGR. Key finding: 95% of contact center AI projects fail — not from algorithm issues, but from data integration gaps where AI can only access ~30% of customer context.
