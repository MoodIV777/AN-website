---
title: Rethinking Freedom, Courage, and Choice in AI Design
date: 2026-03-07
tags: Product Design, AI Philosophy, User Psychology
---
A philosophical exploration of human desires, the scarcity mindset, and how modern internet products often capitalize on information anxiety. It argues that AI products should not simply bombard users with more choices—which only heightens the fear of missing out—but should instead act as a psychological partner. By reducing decision fatigue, providing transparency, and focusing on "better" rather than "more" choices, AI can help users attain a "higher-level mindset" characterized by relaxed, confident, and anxiety-free decision-making.
