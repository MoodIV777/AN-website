title: Global BPO & Call Center Market Outlook
date: 2026-02-01
tags: BPO, Market Research, Cloud, Digital Transformation
---
The global BPO market reached $328B in 2025 and is projected to hit $696B by 2033 (9.9% CAGR). Call center software is the fastest-growing segment at 20.6% CAGR, driven by cloud migration and AI adoption. China's BPO market ($29B in 2024) is growing at 12% — outpacing the global average. By 2028, 70% of customer journeys will start with conversational AI.
